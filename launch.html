Launch Information & Platform Overview

Launch Date

We are excited to announce that our platform will officially launch on March 15, 2025.

After months of development, testing, and refining, we are ready to bring you a fully functional, free platform for books, films, and other creative media. Mark your calendars and stay tuned for further updates!

Platform Overview

Our platform is a centralized, democratic space where anyone can share their creative works, including books, films, and more. The goal is to make publishing accessible for everyone, especially small publishers, independent authors, and filmmakers.

You can access all content for free and also publish your own work for free. Whether you're a small press, an indie filmmaker, or an aspiring author, our platform offers the freedom to share your work without the usual barriers of traditional publishing.

The platform fosters an open and collaborative environment where users can connect, share ideas, and discover new works. Join us in making publishing more inclusive and accessible to everyone.
